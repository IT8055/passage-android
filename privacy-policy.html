Privacy Policy — Passage

Last updated: 4 June 2026

In short: Passage does not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share any of your data. Everything you enter stays on your device, under your control.

1. Who we are

Passage ("the app") is a personal bowel-health diary developed and published by Walker-Jones (walker-jones.eu). For any questions about this policy, contact dev@walker-jones.eu.

2. What data the app handles

The app lets you record entries about your bowel health, which may include Bristol Stool Scale type, stool colour, urgency, pain, symptoms, free-text notes, and optional photos you choose to attach. This information is considered personal health information.

3. Where your data is stored

All data is stored exclusively on your own device, inside the app's private storage. The app has no account system, no login, and no cloud component. We — the developers — never receive, see, or have any access to your entries or photos.

4. Data we collect

None. The app does not collect or transmit any personal data, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or diagnostic information. There are no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries in the app.

5. Sharing your data

The app includes optional Export features (CSV and JSON). When you choose to export, the file is created on your device and you decide what to do with it using Android's standard share sheet — for example, emailing it to your doctor or saving it to your files. This action is always initiated by you, and the data goes only where you send it. We are not involved in and cannot see these transfers.

6. Permissions

If you attach a photo to an entry, the app uses Android's system camera/file picker. Photos you select are stored only within the app on your device. The app does not access your wider photo library, contacts, or location.

7. Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can remove individual entries, or use "Clear All Data" in Settings to erase everything. Uninstalling the app also permanently deletes all app data from your device. Because we never hold your data, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

8. Children

The app is intended for general audiences managing their own health and is not directed at children under 13.

9. Medical disclaimer

Passage is a personal symptom-tracking tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your health.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date above.
